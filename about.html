About

Welcome to The Meme Archive, a website dedicated to storing and sharing memes. This site is a labor of love, created for fun and as a way to showcase my skills as a full stack web developer.

A Senior Project

This website is also serving as my senior project for my Bachelor's degree in Full Stack Web Development. I'm excited to demonstrate my knowledge and skills in designing and building a functional web application.

Just for Fun

While this site is a serious project for my education, it's also a way for me to have fun and express my creativity. I love memes, and I'm excited to share them with the world. I hope you enjoy browsing through the site and finding some laughs along the way.

What to Expect

On this site, you can expect to find a collection of memes, as well as some fun features and tools for interacting with them. I'll be continually updating and improving the site, so be sure to check back often for new content and features.

Thanks for Visiting!

Thanks for taking the time to visit The Meme Archive. I hope you have a great time exploring the site, and I look forward to hearing your feedback and suggestions.
